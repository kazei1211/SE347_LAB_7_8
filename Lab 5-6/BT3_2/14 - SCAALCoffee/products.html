Where we began

Anders Carlson lived a sheltered
life in a small fishing village in
Marstrand, Sweden. As a child,
he listened to stories of his
grandfather Oluf 's adventures
and expeditions. Often his
grandfather would bring back
exotic coffees for the family to
enjoy. Upon his graduation
Anders left home to see the world.
He worked all kinds of odd jobs.
He worked in Parisian cafes and
Italian espresso bars. He worked
in the coffee fields of Brazil and
New Guinea. And in a few years,
Anders learned the art of selecting
the perfect coffee bean and roasting
it to perfection. He returned home
in 1980 to open the first SCAAL in
Stockholm, Sweden.

By creating his own unique blends of coffee and a stimulating atmosphere, SCAAL quickly became the place to gather " day or night " not only in Stockholm, Sweden, but 670 cities around the world.
